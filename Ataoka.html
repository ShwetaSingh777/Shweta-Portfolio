ATAOKA

Adobe Illustrator
Brief By
Self-initiated
Mockups Credit
Microsoft Bing

CHALLENGE
Ataoka, a newly launched Japanese non-alcoholic wine brand focused on people with depression wants a peaceful yet powerful packaging design.

INSIGHT
Japanese people always hide their emotions and try not to show their real crazy side that increases unhappiness inside them.

IDEA
A packaging design that normalizes a crazy life with the tagline- 'What is crazy from outside, is beautiful from inside.'

EXECUTION
A 14 stanza long Ramune (a form of ancient Japanese poetry) was formed, accompanied by 7 illustrations. They were arranged on a hexagonal box. The wine is inside a butterfly-shaped (symbolizes transformation) bottle. The work is accompanied by a promotional video for the company.
