Hi there,

Shweta is an illustrator and animator based in New Delhi, India. Along with sketching to calm herself, she has always enjoyed storytelling. Disney movies and the anime world have been her greatest source of inspiration.

Education

She is currently a graphic design student at the International School of Design, New Delhi (2021-2025). She has further improved her skills and craft through Drawabox and Domestika courses.
